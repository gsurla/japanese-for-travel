Review Day 3 (5 minutes)

Let's quickly review what we learned yesterday:

Hiragana Review:

S-row: さ (sa), し (shi), す (su), せ (se), そ (so)

T-row: た (ta), ち (chi), つ (tsu), て (te), と (to)

Question Phrases Review:

  • これは何ですか？ (Kore wa nan desu ka?) - What is this?
  • どこですか？ (Doko desu ka?) - Where is it?
  • いくらですか？ (Ikura desu ka?) - How much is it?

Essential Travel Vocabulary (15 minutes)

Today we'll learn some of the most important vocabulary words you'll need during your travels in Japan.

Places

Japanese Romaji English
空港 kūkō airport
駅 eki station
ホテル hoteru hotel
レストラン resutoran restaurant
トイレ toire toilet/bathroom
コンビニ konbini convenience store
銀行 ginkō bank

Transportation

Japanese Romaji English
電車 densha train
バス basu bus
タクシー takushī taxi
地下鉄 chikatetsu subway
切符 kippu ticket

Essential Items

Japanese Romaji English
お金 o-kane money
パスポート pasupōto passport
カード kādo card (credit/debit)
鍵 kagi key
携帯電話 keitai denwa mobile phone
Wi-Fi wai-fai Wi-Fi

Using the Vocabulary in Context (10 minutes)

Now let's practice using these vocabulary words in simple sentences and questions.

Location Questions:

Japanese Romaji English
トイレはどこですか？ Toire wa doko desu ka? Where is the toilet/bathroom?
駅はどこですか？ Eki wa doko desu ka? Where is the station?
コンビニはどこですか？ Konbini wa doko desu ka? Where is the convenience store?

Price Questions:

Japanese Romaji English
切符はいくらですか？ Kippu wa ikura desu ka? How much is the ticket?
タクシーはいくらですか？ Takushī wa ikura desu ka? How much is the taxi?

Simple Statements:

Japanese Romaji English
これはパスポートです。 Kore wa pasupōto desu. This is a passport.
あれは銀行です。 Are wa ginkō desu. That is a bank.
お金がありません。 O-kane ga arimasen. I don't have money.
