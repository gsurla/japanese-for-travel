Review of Day 19 (5 minutes)

Let's quickly review the technology vocabulary and phrases we learned yesterday:

  • Technology words: スマホ (sumaho - smartphone), Wi-Fi (wai-fai - Wi-Fi), 充電 (juuden - charging), パスワード (pasuwaado - password), アプリ (apuri - app), 電子マネー (denshi manee - electronic money)
  • Key phrases:
    • Wi-Fiはありますか？ (Wai-fai wa arimasu ka?) - Is there Wi-Fi?
    • スマホを充電してもいいですか？ (Sumaho wo juuden shite mo ii desu ka?) - May I charge my smartphone?
    • 電子マネーは使えますか？ (Denshi manee wa tsukaemasu ka?) - Can I use electronic money?

Comprehensive Review (20 minutes)

Today, we'll review the most important vocabulary and phrases from our 20-day Japanese course. This will help reinforce what you've learned and prepare you for your trip to Japan.

Essential Greetings and Expressions:

  • こんにちは (konnichiwa) - Hello
  • おはようございます (ohayou gozaimasu) - Good morning
  • こんばんは (konbanwa) - Good evening
  • さようなら (sayounara) - Goodbye
  • ありがとうございます (arigatou gozaimasu) - Thank you
  • すみません (sumimasen) - Excuse me/I'm sorry
  • はい (hai) - Yes
  • いいえ (iie) - No
  • お願いします (onegaishimasu) - Please
  • わかりました (wakarimashita) - I understand

Key Question Words:

  • 何 (nani) - What
  • 誰 (dare) - Who
  • どこ (doko) - Where
  • いつ (itsu) - When
  • どうして (doushite) - Why
  • どうやって (douyatte) - How
  • いくら (ikura) - How much

Essential Phrases by Situation:

Transportation:
  • ＿＿に行きたいです (__ ni ikitai desu) - I want to go to __
  • これは＿＿行きのバスですか？ (Kore wa __ iki no basu desu ka?) - Is this the bus to __?
  • 次の駅は何ですか？ (Tsugi no eki wa nan desu ka?) - What is the next station?
  • タクシーを呼んでください (Takushii wo yonde kudasai) - Please call a taxi
Restaurant:
  • 予約をしたいです (Yoyaku wo shitai desu) - I would like to make a reservation
  • メニューをください (Menyuu wo kudasai) - Please give me the menu
  • おすすめは何ですか？ (Osusume wa nan desu ka?) - What do you recommend?
  • お会計をお願いします (Okaikei wo onegaishimasu) - Bill, please

Travel Preparation Tips (15 minutes)

Let's discuss some practical tips for preparing for your trip to Japan:

1. Essential Items to Pack:

  • パスポート (pasupooto) - passport
    • Make sure it's valid for at least 6 months beyond your stay
    • Keep a photocopy or digital copy separate from the original
  • 現金 (genkin) - cash
    • Japan is still largely a cash-based society
    • Bring some yen with you or exchange money at the airport
    • 10,000-20,000 yen is good for initial expenses
  • クレジットカード (kurejitto kaado) - credit card
  • 変換プラグ (henkan puragu) - adapter plug
  • 常備薬 (joubiiyaku) - regular medications
  • 旅行保険 (ryokou hoken) - travel insurance

2. Useful Apps to Download:

  • Google Maps: For navigation
  • Google Translate: For translation help
  • Japan Transit Planner: For train and subway information
  • Currency converter: For price comparisons
  • Weather app: To check local forecasts

3. Cultural Etiquette Reminders:

  • 靴を脱ぐ (kutsu wo nugu) - removing shoes
  • お辞儀 (ojigi) - bowing
  • 箸のマナー (hashi no manaa) - chopstick etiquette
  • 公共の場でのマナー (koukyou no ba de no manaa) - public etiquette

Practical Japanese for Your Trip (15 minutes)

Let's practice putting together what you've learned in real travel scenarios:

Scenario 1: Arriving at the Airport

Officer: パスポートをください。(Pasupooto wo kudasai.)

Please give me your passport.

You: はい、どうぞ。(Hai, douzo.)

Yes, here you are.

Officer: 日本での滞在期間は？(Nihon de no taizai kikan wa?)

How long will you stay in Japan?

You: 10日間です。(Juu-nichi-kan desu.)

10 days.

Officer: 滞在目的は？(Taizai mokuteki wa?)

What is the purpose of your visit?

You: 観光です。(Kankou desu.)

Tourism.

Scenario 2: Checking into Your Hotel

You: こんにちは。チェックインをお願いします。(Konnichiwa. Chekkuin wo onegaishimasu.)

Hello. I'd like to check in, please.

Staff: お名前をお願いします。(Onamae wo onegaishimasu.)

Your name, please.

You: [Your Name]です。予約があります。(__ desu. Yoyaku ga arimasu.)

I'm [Your Name]. I have a reservation.

Staff: パスポートをお願いします。(Pasupooto wo onegaishimasu.)

Your passport, please.

You: はい、どうぞ。Wi-Fiはありますか？(Hai, douzo. Wi-Fi wa arimasu ka?)

Yes, here you are. Is there Wi-Fi?
