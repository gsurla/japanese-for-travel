Useful Phrases for Ordering

すみません

Sumimasen

Excuse me

Use this to get a server's attention.

メニューをお願いします

Menyū o onegaishimasu

Menu, please

これをください

Kore o kudasai

This, please

You can point to a menu item and use this phrase.

水をください

Mizu o kudasai

Water, please

おすすめは何ですか

Osusume wa nan desu ka

What do you recommend?

お会計をお願いします

O-kaikei o onegaishimasu

The bill, please

おいしいです

Oishii desu

It's delicious

Cultural Notes: Restaurant Etiquette

Saying "Itadakimasu" and "Gochisōsama"

いただきます

Itadakimasu

"I gratefully receive"

Say this before starting your meal, similar to saying "bon appétit" or giving thanks for the food.

ごちそうさま(でした)

Gochisōsama (deshita)

"Thank you for the meal"

Say this after finishing your meal to express gratitude.

No Tipping Culture

In Japan, tipping is not customary and may even cause confusion. Service charges are already included in your bill.

Practice: Restaurant Role-Play

Ordering Food Scenario

Practice with a partner or imagine yourself in these situations:

Customer: すみません (Sumimasen) - Excuse me

Server: はい、いらっしゃいませ (Hai, irasshaimase) - Yes, welcome

Customer: メニューをお願いします (Menyū o onegaishimasu) - Menu, please

Server: はい、どうぞ (Hai, dōzo) - Yes, here you are

Customer: これをください (Kore o kudasai) - This, please

Server: かしこまりました (Kashikomarimashita) - Understood/Certainly
