About This Guide

This comprehensive Japanese for Travel guide includes a 20-day curriculum with 30-minute daily lessons and reference sheets for common travel situations. Whether you're planning your first trip to Japan or returning for another visit, these materials will help you communicate more effectively and enhance your travel experience.

The curriculum is designed to be accessible to beginners with no prior Japanese knowledge. Each day includes vocabulary, phrases, and practice suggestions that can be completed in just 30 minutes.

The reference sheets are organized by situation and include phonetic pronunciations (romanized Japanese) alongside the Japanese phrases, making them easy to use during your travels.
