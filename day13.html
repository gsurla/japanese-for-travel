Review Day 12: Restaurant & Ordering Phrases

Let's quickly review some key phrases from yesterday about restaurants:

  • 「お願いします」(onegaishimasu) - Please/I'd like this
  • 「ご注文は？」(go-chuumon wa?) - May I take your order?
  • 「これをください」(kore o kudasai) - This, please
  • 「お勘定お願いします」(o-kanjou onegaishimasu) - Check, please

Understanding Izakaya Culture

Izakayas are Japanese-style pubs that serve both drinks and food. They're perfect for casual dining, socializing after work, and experiencing Japanese drinking culture.

Key Characteristics of Izakayas:

  • Ordering style: Food and drinks are typically ordered in small portions and shared among the group
  • Seating options: Counter seats, tables, or traditional tatami mat areas
  • Service call: Use "すみません" (sumimasen) to call staff's attention
  • Cover charge: Many izakayas charge a 席料 (sekiryou) or お通し (otōshi) - a small cover charge that includes a small appetizer

Cultural Note:

It's customary in Japan to pour drinks for others in your group rather than for yourself. This is especially true in business settings. When someone pours a drink for you, it's polite to hold your glass with both hands.

Useful Izakaya & Bar Phrases

乾杯！
Kanpai!
Cheers!
Used when raising glasses for a toast
生ビールをください。
Nama biiru o kudasai.
Draft beer, please.
For ordering a draft beer
おすすめは何ですか？
Osusume wa nan desu ka?
What do you recommend?
For asking staff for recommendations
もう一杯お願いします。
Mō ippai onegaishimasu.
One more, please.
For ordering another drink
お通しは何ですか？
Otōshi wa nan desu ka?
What is the cover charge/appetizer?
To ask about the mandatory appetizer/cover charge
禁煙席をお願いします。
Kin'enseki o onegaishimasu.
Non-smoking section, please.
For requesting a non-smoking table
お会計をお願いします。
O-kaikei o onegaishimasu.
The bill, please.
To ask for the check
別々で支払います。
Betsu betsu de shiharaimasu.
We'll pay separately.
To request separate checks

Common Drinking Expressions

お酒に弱いです。
Osake ni yowai desu.
I can't drink much alcohol (lit: I'm weak with alcohol).
お酒は飲みません。
Osake wa nomimasen.
I don't drink alcohol.
ノンアルコールはありますか？
Non-arukōru wa arimasu ka?
Do you have non-alcoholic options?
