20-Day Japanese Travel Language Learning Curriculum

This curriculum is designed for travelers who want to learn essential Japanese in just 30 minutes per day. Each day builds on previous knowledge while focusing on practical travel situations. Click on any day to access the detailed lesson content.

Before You Begin

  • This curriculum assumes no prior knowledge of Japanese
  • Each day includes a 5-minute review of previous material
  • Practice pronunciation using online resources or language apps
  • Try to practice with audio whenever possible

Week 1 – Foundation & Basic Travel Phrases

Day 1: Japanese Writing System & Basic Greetings

5 min: Introduction to Japanese writing systems (Hiragana, Katakana, Kanji)
10 min: Learn 5 essential greetings:
  • こんにちは (Konnichiwa) - Hello
  • おはようございます (Ohayou gozaimasu) - Good morning
  • こんばんは (Konbanwa) - Good evening
  • さようなら (Sayounara) - Goodbye
  • ありがとうございます (Arigatou gozaimasu) - Thank you
10 min: Practice pronunciation and basic conversation flow
5 min: Learn to introduce yourself: わたしは [name] です (Watashi wa [name] desu) - I am [name]

Day 2: Basic Hiragana & Numbers 1-10

5 min: Review greetings from Day 1
10 min: Learn first 10 hiragana characters: あ、い、う、え、お、か、き、く、け、こ
10 min: Learn numbers 1-10:
  • 一 (ichi) - one
  • 二 (ni) - two
  • 三 (san) - three
  • 四 (yon/shi) - four
  • 五 (go) - five
  • 六 (roku) - six
  • 七 (nana/shichi) - seven
  • 八 (hachi) - eight
  • 九 (kyuu/ku) - nine
  • 十 (juu) - ten
5 min: Practice counting and recognizing numbers

Day 3: More Hiragana & Basic Questions

5 min: Review hiragana and numbers from Day 2
10 min: Learn 10 more hiragana: さ、し、す、せ、そ、た、ち、つ、て、と
10 min: Learn basic question phrases:
  • これは何ですか？ (Kore wa nan desu ka?) - What is this?
  • どこですか？ (Doko desu ka?) - Where is it?
  • いくらですか？ (Ikura desu ka?) - How much is it?
5 min: Practice forming questions and answers

Day 4: Essential Travel Vocabulary

5 min: Review hiragana and questions from Day 3
10 min: Learn 10 more hiragana: な、に、ぬ、ね、の、は、ひ、ふ、へ、ほ
15 min: Learn essential travel vocabulary:
  • ホテル (hoteru) - hotel
  • 空港 (kuukou) - airport
  • 駅 (eki) - station
  • トイレ (toire) - toilet/bathroom
  • 水 (mizu) - water
  • お金 (okane) - money
  • パスポート (pasupooto) - passport

Day 5: Basic Sentence Structure & Polite Expressions

5 min: Review vocabulary from Day 4
10 min: Learn 10 more hiragana: ま、み、む、め、も、や、ゆ、よ、ら、り
10 min: Learn basic sentence structure:
  • [Topic] は [Comment] です。([Topic] wa [Comment] desu.)
  • Example: これはホテルです。 (Kore wa hoteru desu.) - This is a hotel.
5 min: Learn polite expressions:
  • すみません (Sumimasen) - Excuse me/I'm sorry
  • お願いします (Onegaishimasu) - Please

Day 6: Completing Hiragana & Basic Verbs

5 min: Review sentence structure from Day 5
10 min: Learn remaining hiragana: る、れ、ろ、わ、を、ん + basic combinations
15 min: Learn essential verbs:
  • 行きます (ikimasu) - to go
  • 食べます (tabemasu) - to eat
  • 飲みます (nomimasu) - to drink
  • 見ます (mimasu) - to see/watch
  • 買います (kaimasu) - to buy

Day 7: Review Week 1 & Practice Conversations

10 min: Review hiragana
10 min: Review numbers and essential vocabulary
10 min: Practice basic conversations combining all elements learned this week
Bonus: Try writing your name in katakana if time permits

Week 2 – Practical Travel Situations

Day 8: Transportation Vocabulary & Phrases

5 min: Review verbs from Day 6
10 min: Learn transportation vocabulary:
  • バス (basu) - bus
  • 電車 (densha) - train
  • タクシー (takushii) - taxi
  • 地下鉄 (chikatetsu) - subway
  • 切符 (kippu) - ticket
15 min: Learn useful transportation phrases:
  • ＿＿行きのバスはどこですか？ (__ iki no basu wa doko desu ka?) - Where is the bus to __?
  • ＿＿までいくらですか？ (__ made ikura desu ka?) - How much is it to __?
  • 一枚お願いします (Ichimai onegaishimasu) - One ticket, please

Day 9: Restaurant Vocabulary & Ordering Food

5 min: Review transportation phrases from Day 8
10 min: Learn restaurant vocabulary:
  • レストラン (resutoran) - restaurant
  • メニュー (menyuu) - menu
  • 水 (mizu) - water
  • お茶 (ocha) - tea
  • ビール (biiru) - beer
15 min: Learn ordering phrases:
  • ＿＿をください (__ wo kudasai) - Please give me __
  • おすすめは何ですか？ (Osusume wa nan desu ka?) - What do you recommend?
  • お会計をお願いします (Okaikei wo onegaishimasu) - Bill, please

Day 10: Hotel Check-in & Accommodation Phrases

5 min: Review restaurant phrases from Day 9
10 min: Learn hotel vocabulary:
  • フロント (furonto) - front desk
  • 部屋 (heya) - room
  • 鍵 (kagi) - key
  • エレベーター (erebeetaa) - elevator
  • 予約 (yoyaku) - reservation
15 min: Learn hotel phrases:
  • チェックインをお願いします (Chekkuin wo onegaishimasu) - I'd like to check in, please
  • 予約があります (Yoyaku ga arimasu) - I have a reservation
  • Wi-Fiはありますか？ (Wi-Fi wa arimasu ka?) - Is there Wi-Fi?

Day 11: Directions & Location Words

5 min: Review hotel phrases from Day 10
15 min: Learn direction vocabulary:
  • 右 (migi) - right
  • 左 (hidari) - left
  • まっすぐ (massugu) - straight
  • 曲がる (magaru) - to turn
  • 近く (chikaku) - near
  • 遠く (tooku) - far
  • 隣 (tonari) - next to
10 min: Practice asking for directions:
  • ＿＿はどこですか？ (__ wa doko desu ka?) - Where is __?
  • ＿＿までどうやって行きますか？ (__ made douyatte ikimasu ka?) - How do I get to __?
  • 道に迷いました (Michi ni mayoimashita) - I am lost

Day 12: Shopping Vocabulary & Phrases

5 min: Review direction vocabulary from Day 11
15 min: Learn shopping vocabulary:
  • お土産 (omiyage) - souvenir
  • 安い (yasui) - cheap/inexpensive
  • 高い (takai) - expensive
  • サイズ (saizu) - size
  • 色 (iro) - color
  • 試着 (shichaku) - fitting/trying on
10 min: Practice shopping phrases:
  • これをください (Kore wo kudasai) - I'll take this
  • 試着してもいいですか？ (Shichaku shite mo ii desu ka?) - May I try this on?
  • もっと安いのはありますか？ (Motto yasui no wa arimasu ka?) - Do you have anything cheaper?

Day 13: Sightseeing Vocabulary & Cultural Sites

5 min: Review shopping phrases from Day 12
15 min: Learn sightseeing vocabulary:
  • 神社 (jinja) - shrine
  • お寺 (otera) - temple
  • 城 (shiro) - castle
  • 博物館 (hakubutsukan) - museum
  • 公園 (kouen) - park
  • 写真 (shashin) - photograph
  • 入場料 (nyuujouryou) - admission fee
10 min: Practice sightseeing phrases:
  • 写真を撮ってもらえますか？ (Shashin wo totte moraemasu ka?) - Could you take my picture?
  • 開館時間は何時ですか？ (Kaikan jikan wa nanji desu ka?) - What time does it open?
  • 入場料はいくらですか？ (Nyuujouryou wa ikura desu ka?) - How much is the admission fee?

Day 14: Review Week 2 & Practice Scenarios

15 min: Review transportation, restaurant, hotel, and shopping phrases
15 min: Role-play practice: ordering at a restaurant, checking into a hotel, asking for directions

Week 3 – Expanding Vocabulary & Specific Situations

Day 15: Onsen (Japanese Bath) Vocabulary & Etiquette

5 min: Review sightseeing vocabulary from Day 13
15 min: Learn onsen vocabulary:
  • 温泉 (onsen) - hot spring
  • 浴場 (yokujou) - bath house
  • タオル (taoru) - towel
  • 脱衣所 (datsuijo) - changing room
  • 洗い場 (araiba) - washing area
10 min: Learn onsen etiquette phrases:
  • 靴を脱いでください (Kutsu wo nuide kudasai) - Please take off your shoes
  • 体を先に洗ってください (Karada wo saki ni aratte kudasai) - Please wash your body first
  • 入れ墨/タトゥーはだめです (Irezumi/tatuu wa dame desu) - Tattoos are not allowed

Day 16: Emergency Phrases & Health Vocabulary

5 min: Review onsen vocabulary from Day 15
15 min: Learn emergency vocabulary:
  • 助けて (tasukete) - help
  • 警察 (keisatsu) - police
  • 病院 (byouin) - hospital
  • 薬局 (yakkyoku) - pharmacy
  • 痛い (itai) - painful/hurts
  • 熱 (netsu) - fever
  • アレルギー (arerugii) - allergy
10 min: Practice emergency phrases:
  • 具合が悪いです (Guai ga warui desu) - I feel sick
  • 警察を呼んでください (Keisatsu wo yonde kudasai) - Please call the police
  • 英語を話せる人はいますか？ (Eigo wo hanaseru hito wa imasu ka?) - Is there someone who speaks English?

Day 17: Weather & Seasonal Expressions

5 min: Review emergency phrases from Day 16
15 min: Learn weather vocabulary:
  • 天気 (tenki) - weather
  • 雨 (ame) - rain
  • 晴れ (hare) - sunny
  • 雪 (yuki) - snow
  • 暑い (atsui) - hot (weather)
  • 寒い (samui) - cold (weather)
  • 涼しい (suzushii) - cool/refreshing
10 min: Practice weather-related phrases:
  • 今日の天気はどうですか？ (Kyou no tenki wa dou desu ka?) - How is the weather today?
  • 明日は雨が降りますか？ (Ashita wa ame ga furimasu ka?) - Will it rain tomorrow?
  • とても暑いです (Totemo atsui desu) - It's very hot

Day 18: Small Talk & Basic Conversation

5 min: Review weather expressions from Day 17
15 min: Learn small talk vocabulary:
  • 出身 (shusshin) - hometown/origin
  • 仕事 (shigoto) - job/work
  • 家族 (kazoku) - family
  • 趣味 (shumi) - hobby
  • 好き (suki) - like
  • 楽しい (tanoshii) - fun/enjoyable
10 min: Practice small talk phrases:
  • どこから来ましたか？ (Doko kara kimashita ka?) - Where are you from?
  • 日本は初めてですか？ (Nihon wa hajimete desu ka?) - Is this your first time in Japan?
  • 日本料理は好きですか？ (Nihon ryouri wa suki desu ka?) - Do you like Japanese food?

Day 19: Technology & Modern Travel Needs

5 min: Review small talk phrases from Day 18
15 min: Learn technology vocabulary:
  • Wi-Fi (wai-fai)
  • 充電器 (juudenki) - charger
  • パスワード (pasuwaado) - password
  • インターネット (intaanetto) - internet
  • スマホ (sumaho) - smartphone
  • カメラ (kamera) - camera
10 min: Practice technology phrases:
  • Wi-Fiのパスワードは何ですか？ (Wi-Fi no pasuwaado wa nan desu ka?) - What is the Wi-Fi password?
  • 充電してもいいですか？ (Juuden shite mo ii desu ka?) - May I charge (my device)?
  • 写真を撮ってもいいですか？ (Shashin wo totte mo ii desu ka?) - May I take a photo?

Day 20: Final Review & Travel Simulation

10 min: Review emergency phrases and small talk
10 min: Review restaurant, hotel, and transportation phrases
10 min: Full travel day simulation combining all learned phrases
Bonus: Create a personalized cheat sheet of phrases you find most useful
