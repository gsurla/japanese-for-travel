20-Day Japanese Travel Language Learning Curriculum

This curriculum is designed for travelers who want to learn essential Japanese in just 30 minutes per day. Each day builds on previous knowledge while focusing on practical travel situations. Click on any day to access the detailed lesson content.

Before You Begin

  • This curriculum assumes no prior knowledge of Japanese
  • Each day includes a 5-minute review of previous material
  • Practice pronunciation using online resources or language apps
  • Try to practice with audio whenever possible

Week 1 – Foundation & Basic Travel Phrases

Day 1: Japanese Writing System & Basic Greetings

5 min: Introduction to Japanese writing systems (Hiragana, Katakana, Kanji)
10 min: Learn 5 essential greetings:
  • こんにちは (Konnichiwa) - Hello
  • おはようございます (Ohayou gozaimasu) - Good morning
  • こんばんは (Konbanwa) - Good evening
  • さようなら (Sayounara) - Goodbye
  • ありがとうございます (Arigatou gozaimasu) - Thank you
10 min: Practice pronunciation and basic conversation flow
5 min: Learn to introduce yourself: わたしは [name] です (Watashi wa [name] desu) - I am [name]

Day 2: Basic Hiragana & Numbers 1-10

5 min: Review greetings from Day 1
10 min: Learn first 10 hiragana characters: あ、い、う、え、お、か、き、く、け、こ
10 min: Learn numbers 1-10:
  • 一 (ichi) - one
  • 二 (ni) - two
  • 三 (san) - three
  • 四 (yon/shi) - four
  • 五 (go) - five
  • 六 (roku) - six
  • 七 (nana/shichi) - seven
  • 八 (hachi) - eight
  • 九 (kyuu/ku) - nine
  • 十 (juu) - ten
5 min: Practice counting and recognizing numbers

Day 3: More Hiragana & Basic Questions

5 min: Review hiragana and numbers from Day 2
10 min: Learn 10 more hiragana: さ、し、す、せ、そ、た、ち、つ、て、と
10 min: Learn basic question phrases:
  • これは何ですか？ (Kore wa nan desu ka?) - What is this?
  • どこですか？ (Doko desu ka?) - Where is it?
  • いくらですか？ (Ikura desu ka?) - How much is it?
5 min: Practice forming questions and answers

Day 4: Essential Travel Vocabulary

5 min: Review hiragana and questions from Day 3
10 min: Learn 10 more hiragana: な、に、ぬ、ね、の、は、ひ、ふ、へ、ほ
15 min: Learn essential travel vocabulary:
  • ホテル (hoteru) - hotel
  • 空港 (kuukou) - airport
  • 駅 (eki) - station
  • トイレ (toire) - toilet/bathroom
  • 水 (mizu) - water
  • お金 (okane) - money
  • パスポート (pasupooto) - passport

Day 5: Basic Sentence Structure & Polite Expressions

5 min: Review vocabulary from Day 4
10 min: Learn 10 more hiragana: ま、み、む、め、も、や、ゆ、よ、ら、り
10 min: Learn basic sentence structure:
  • [Topic] は [Comment] です。([Topic] wa [Comment] desu.)
  • Example: これはホテルです。 (Kore wa hoteru desu.) - This is a hotel.
5 min: Learn polite expressions:
  • すみません (Sumimasen) - Excuse me/I'm sorry
  • お願いします (Onegaishimasu) - Please

Day 6: Completing Hiragana & Basic Verbs

5 min: Review sentence structure from Day 5
10 min: Learn remaining hiragana: る、れ、ろ、わ、を、ん + basic combinations
15 min: Learn essential verbs:
  • 行きます (ikimasu) - to go
  • 食べます (tabemasu) - to eat
  • 飲みます (nomimasu) - to drink
  • 見ます (mimasu) - to see/watch
  • 買います (kaimasu) - to buy

Day 7: Review Week 1 & Practice Conversations

10 min: Review hiragana
10 min: Review numbers and essential vocabulary
10 min: Practice basic conversations combining all elements learned this week
Bonus: Try writing your name in katakana if time permits

Week 2 – Practical Travel Situations

Day 8: Transportation Vocabulary & Phrases

5 min: Review verbs from Day 6
10 min: Learn transportation vocabulary:
  • バス (basu) - bus
  • 電車 (densha) - train
  • タクシー (takushii) - taxi
  • 地下鉄 (chikatetsu) - subway
  • 切符 (kippu) - ticket
15 min: Learn useful transportation phrases:
  • ＿＿行きのバスはどこですか？ (__ iki no basu wa doko desu ka?) - Where is the bus to __?
  • ＿＿までいくらですか？ (__ made ikura desu ka?) - How much is it to __?
  • 一枚お願いします (Ichimai onegaishimasu) - One ticket, please

Day 9: Restaurant Vocabulary & Ordering Food

5 min: Review transportation phrases from Day 8
10 min: Learn restaurant vocabulary:
  • レストラン (resutoran) - restaurant
  • メニュー (menyuu) - menu
  • 水 (mizu) - water
  • お茶 (ocha) - tea
  • ビール (biiru) - beer
15 min: Learn ordering phrases:
  • ＿＿をください (__ wo kudasai) - Please give me __
  • おすすめは何ですか？ (Osusume wa nan desu ka?) - What do you recommend?
  • お会計をお願いします (Okaikei wo onegaishimasu) - Bill, please

Day 10: Hotel Check-in & Accommodation Phrases

5 min: Review restaurant phrases from Day 9
10 min: Learn hotel vocabulary:
  • フロント (furonto) - front desk
  • 部屋 (heya) - room
  • 鍵 (kagi) - key
  • エレベーター (erebeetaa) - elevator
  • 予約 (yoyaku) - reservation
15 min: Learn hotel phrases:
  • チェックインをお願いします (Chekkuin wo onegaishimasu) - I'd like to check in, please
  • 予約があります (Yoyaku ga arimasu) - I have a reservation
  • Wi-Fiはありますか？ (Wi-Fi wa arimasu ka?) - Is there Wi-Fi?
